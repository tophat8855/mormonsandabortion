Mormons have abortions.

If you are here because you are a Mormon with the prospect of having to choose to continue or end a pregnancy, you are not alone.

If you are here to understand why some Mormons choose abortion, click the links and read and listen. It is very likely that someone in your congregation has had an abortion.

Some stories have regrets, some do not. An abortion does not define a person; we are all much more, especially to God.
